About Us

THRONE Project is an international engineering company. We design and install automation systems for homes, offices and industrial buildings. These systems are often called Smart Home, Smart Office or Smart Building.

Our business is built around the THRONE 3D Control technology — a breakthrough interface for automation systems based on the 3D model of a building. We offer clients the combination of this revolutionary product with high quality and reliability of the equipment produced under European standard KNX.

THRONE Project has more than 10 years of experience of different scale projects implementation: from Smart Home in residential houses (villa or appartment) to automation of huge commercial facilities (malls, offices, business centers, etc.).

About Us

THRONE Project is an international engineering company. We design and install automation systems for homes, offices and industrial buildings. These systems are often called Smart Home, Smart Office or Smart Building.

Our business is built around the THRONE 3D Control technology — a breakthrough interface for automation systems based on the 3D model of a building. We offer clients the combination of this revolutionary product with high quality and reliability of the equipment produced under European standard KNX.

THRONE Project has more than 10 years of experience of different scale projects implementation: from Smart Home in residential houses (villa or appartment) to automation of huge commercial facilities (malls, offices, business centers, etc.).

What differs us from other companies installing Smart Home systems?

Once we thought: why in our time of high technologies it is so difficult to order a Smart Home system? You can buy anything, without leaving your chair, but the design and installation of Smart Home turns into a complicated and confusing issue! And we understood that this industry lacks standards.

All companies work in their own way, all projects are done differently. Even the term 'smart home' is used each time for something different. The result is that each new project is like climbing a huge mountain. A hard way, which is repeatedly traversed again.

We used an approach that once made a revolution in car production: we made an assembly line for Smart homes. At the basis of any project underlie basic technological components, similar for all. Then we add individual characteristics, which are different for different projects and depend on client's demands.

 
 

We have created simple algorithms, which made life easier for a customer, and installer of a Smart Home system. Earlier the order process of a system looked like assembling a puzzle. We made it similar to buying a car or tour packages: here is the base, here are the options, here are additional services.

We will not impose numerous meetings and presentations whose sole purpose is to estimate how much money you can 'shake out' from the customer. We evaluate the project and prepare specifications remotely, make free demo version, and at the time of signing the contract the client is already 100% sure what his 'smart home' will look like.

  

Our Principles

Rationalism

It's time to stop presenting 'Smart home' as mischievous antics of hilarious wizards. No 'clap your hands and turn on the light'. No 'refrigerator will order food for you'. Those fairy tales made many of reasonable people sceptic regarding 'smart home' systems

We offer reasonable, affordable and really needed systems: control of lighting and blinds, climatic equipment, intercoms and video surveillance, security systems. All this is controlled from a mobile device using THRONE user interface, the best one on the market.

Velocity

Installation of 'Smart Home' should not take longer than building a house. The market is full of companies that do home automation projects for many months, and sometimes more than a year. We have simplified, unified and accelerated the procedures of order, design and installation of Smart Home system. Also, due to 3D THRONE interface, the customer get the full understanding of his system yet at the stage of demo version.

Standards

Unification of business processes and production cycle. The process of designing and installation of Smart Home systems in THRONE Project is a properly debugged algorithm in which there is a clear time and place for every action, document, person. We are not inventing on the fly, but achieve a guaranteed result.

We are working to make 'smart systems' become rational choice of a person, but not a pampering or entertainment. We want the building automation to become mainstream, and its benefits to be enjoyed by billions of people. We believe that our approach to the business will completely change the market.
Mikhail Kulikov, Director of THRONE Project
